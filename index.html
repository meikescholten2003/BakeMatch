About BakeMatch

BakeMatch shows how much you can cook with just the ingredients already in your kitchen. Many people have ingredients at home but don’t know what to make with them. Most recipe sites feel complicated or don’t match what users already have. BakeMatch solves this by showing simple recipes based on the ingredients in your kitchen. It turns cooking into something quick and fun and helps reduce waste at the same time. The platform brings together design, creativity and sustainability in a clear and easy way.

Reduce waste by using the ingredients you already have.

Save time by getting recipe suggestions that fit your kitchen.

Make cooking easy, creative and stress free.
